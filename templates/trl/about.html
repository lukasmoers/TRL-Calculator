About TRL


What is TRL

The Technology Readiness Level (TRL) scale was created by NASA and further developed by the AFRL to measure the maturity of new technologies, help monitor their progress and advance their development. It can inform discussions and decisions around a common point of reference, keeping stakeholders on the same page at all times.


Additionally, this TRL calculator can help you build a progress roadmap for your technology, from initial idea all the way to usable product. This way, you will always know exactly what you need to do, to get your project to the next level.

How it works

Technology readiness is measured in nine discrete levels, based on the completion of specific requirements in your project's research, development and deployment process.


For commercial products, reaching TRL 9 means that an actual system has been proven in its operational environment, whereas for theoretical concepts or research, you can work towards an experimental proof of concept for TRL 3.
