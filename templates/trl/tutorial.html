How to use

Watch these videos to learn how to make the most of this TRL Calculator:

Create a project


You can create a new project from the homepage or your portofolio. You can then customise the calculator according to your project's needs, and begin the TRL calculations.

Update a project


Your portfolio is where you can find all of your projects, to monitor, update or delete them. As you update the completion percentage of different TRL requirements, the interactive roadmap changes colour to reflect your project's current TRL.

View/Export a report


Your project's TRL report contains an overview of its readiness level, and guidance for getting to the next TRL. You can export this to PDF format, to easily download or print it.
